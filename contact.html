Good to know

Frequently asked

How far do you travel?

We're based in Leicester and travel across all of England — including London, Birmingham, Manchester, Nottingham and beyond.

Are your pancakes really eggless?

Yes — 100% eggless, every time. We also offer vegan and nut-free options; just let us know your requirements.

Can I combine services?

Absolutely. Many clients pair the mini pancake and waffle carts, and add chaat or chai for the full experience.

How far in advance should I book?

The earlier the better, especially for weekend and wedding-season dates. Get in touch and we'll check availability for your day.
